## Context

Doyaken has a prompt library in `.doyaken/prompts/library/` with methodology files used by phases and skills. External tools have built prompt marketplaces and sharing ecosystems that may reveal patterns worth adopting.

## Objective

Research AIPRM and PromptHub to understand how they organize, version, share, and discover prompts. Identify features that could improve doyaken's prompt library system.

## Sources

- https://www.aiprm.com/
- https://www.prompthub.us/

## Research Questions

1. How do they categorize and tag prompts?
2. What prompt templating/variable systems do they use?
3. How do they handle prompt versioning and updates?
4. Do they have quality scoring or community ratings?
5. How do users discover relevant prompts?
6. What import/export formats do they support?

## Output

A list of concrete suggestions, improvements, and features to steal for doyaken's prompt library, formatted as potential task descriptions ready to be turned into new tasks.
